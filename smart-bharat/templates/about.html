About Smart Bharat

Making government services simple, accessible, and understandable for every Indian citizen — powered by Generative AI.

Our Mission

Bridging Citizens & Government, One Conversation at a Time

Millions of Indians struggle to navigate complex government processes — confusing forms, scattered information, and long queues. Smart Bharat was built to change that. Using Google's Gemini AI, we translate bureaucratic complexity into plain, actionable guidance — in both English and Hindi — so that every citizen, regardless of literacy or technical skill, can access the services they deserve.
